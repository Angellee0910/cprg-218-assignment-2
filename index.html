FAQ

To brew a flavorful cup of black tea, start by selecting high-quality tea leaves. Then, steep the tea in water heated to 90-95 degrees Celsius for 3-5 minutes, using approximately 2 grams of tea leaves per 250 milliliters of water. Finally, add sugar or milk according to personal preference.

The benefits of drinking tea regularly include boosting alertness, antioxidation, aiding digestion, protecting the heart, enhancing immunity, managing weight, and improving breath.

"Gongfu Cha" is a traditional Chinese tea brewing method that emphasizes precision, ritual, and tasting high-quality teas. It focuses on details such as tea ware selection, tea quantity, water temperature, and steeping time, while also valuing a serene atmosphere and mindful tea appreciation.
